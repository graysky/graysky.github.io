How to Prepare

Fundamentals

  • Data Structures - Arrays, Maps, Trees, Graphs etc.
  • Algorithms - Sorting, searching, Big-O
  • Comfort with coding - practice! (bonus if not C++)

Real World

  • How the web works - HTTP, SSL, DNS, HTML/CSS, JS, etc.
  • Tools - git, IDE/editor, debugger, command line, Linux, Node/Rails/Django
  • Teams - software development is a team sport

Side Projects FTW

  • Build and ship something!
  • Learn a lot by building & owning it
  • Demonstrates passion & determination

How to Find an Internship

Already Have Had an Internship

Stand out

  • Highlight projects (not just classes/GPA)
  • Demonstrate rate of learning
  • Tell a story - like your college application
  • What would you be excited to work on?

Beyond Career Fairs

  • LinkedIn, Greenhorn Connect, AngelList, Google Ventures, VentureFizz
  • Email and apply directly. Follow-up

What to Look For

  • Research companies beyond "they use Java". Use Glassdoor
  • What projects do interns work on? What have past interns done?
  • Who mentors/coaches interns?
  • What is their business?
  • How often do they deploy? How do decisions get made?
